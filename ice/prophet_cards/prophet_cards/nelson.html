Russell M. Nelson

17th president of the church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints

Born September 9, 1924
Apostle from April 7, 1984
President of the Church from January 14, 2018

"I urge you to devote time each week- for the rest of your lives- to the study of the gospel of Jesus Christ. I promise you that as you do so, you will be blessed with greater understanding and a stronger testimony of the Savior." - Russell M. Nelson
